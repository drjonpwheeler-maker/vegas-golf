VEGAS RULES
🎯 The Basics
Each team of 2 forms a 2-digit number each hole. The lower score is the 1st digit, higher score is the 2nd digit. The team with the lower number wins the difference in points.
Example
Team 1 scores 4 & 5 → number is 45
Team 2 scores 5 & 6 → number is 56
Team 1 wins 56 − 45 = 11 points
🐦 Birdie Rule
If a team makes a birdie (or better), the opposing team's digits flip — their high score becomes the 1st digit.

If BOTH teams birdie, BOTH teams flip.
🦅 Eagle Rule
If either team makes an eagle (2 under par) or better, the points for that hole are doubled.
♿ Handicaps
Players receive 1 stroke on holes where their course handicap is greater than or equal to the hole's difficulty rating. Net scores are used for all Vegas calculations.
